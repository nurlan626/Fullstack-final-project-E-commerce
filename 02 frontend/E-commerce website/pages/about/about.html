Our Story

Founded in 2015, Exclusive is a leading online marketplace in South Asia, with active operations in Bangladesh. With a variety of marketing tools, data solutions, and service support, Exclusive connects over 10,500 sellers and 300 brands with 3 million customers.

The platform hosts over 1 million products, continuously expanding. Exclusive's product range covers many categories, including consumer goods.
